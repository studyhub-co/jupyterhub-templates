About Us Team

About Us

We are passionate about the Project Jupyter ecosystem and are launching a free JupyterHub for everyone. Use this with your students, personal projects, or whatever you want! This site runs on Google Cloud Platform using Kubernetes.

This platform is great for:

Educators

Are you teaching Python, R, or Julia? Our platform runs Jupyter's data-science docker image that comes with these languages and key packages pre-installed, so your students can immediately start coding.

Administrators

Don't waste time setting up your own JupyterHub, use ours or hire us to build yours!

Data Scientists

Working on a data science project? Access your files anywhere, anytime. Your files persist in the cloud.

Building a JupyterLab extension? We can help.

We're building our own education-related JupyterLab extensions and would be excited to help you with your application. JupyterLab extensions are a great way to give access to your application to the large community of Jupyter users.

Need a powerful Jupyter instance in the cloud? We provide instances already connected to GPUs, or with large memory or CPU requirements to fit your needs. Contact us for pricing.

If you're working on an application that requires heavy resource usage, we can help. Maybe you want to always have a plug-n-play instance connected to a GPU so you can do more deep-learning and less configuration. Or maybe you have a project that requires large amounts of memory.

Yes, you can set up your own Jupyter Notebook on AWS or GCP, but there's often a lot of painful configuration issues that slow you down from what you're trying to do: data science.

With StudyHub you're always just one click away from your server.
